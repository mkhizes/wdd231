Site Purpose

To assist,and is aimed at educating new and existing farmers on best practices for pig rearing, feeding,health management and profitability

Because it is dedicated to providing useful insights into modern pig farming. The site will cover essential aspects such as farm setup, breeding, feeding, disease management and market trends. It will serve as a helpful guide for aspiring pig famers looking to start or improve their piggery business

Audience

My audience is the Small Micro Medium Enterprises, who are dealing with pigs in the rural areas as well as in the city. even Commercial business people who operating under Ethekwini Metropolitan Municipality and surrounding areas. I am also targeting the informal farmers who have pigs in small scale as well as the domestic farmers and those who wish to work with supplying or providing pork meat hence it is widely consumed globally.
